* Anneal temperatures for the following PCR: ITS at 55°C and ACT at 60°C.

When the PCR is complete, mix 2μl of each PCR sample with 1 μl of DNA loading dye.

Load the samples into a 1% TBE agarose gel.

Run the gel in TBE buffer at 100V for 40 minutes.

Visualise and photograph the gel under UV-light.

## Sequence PCR product

Once it is confirmed that there is a single PCR product of expected size, prepare the PCR product for sequencing. Refer to the sequencing facility's guidelines for sample preparation and shipment.

## DNA sequence analysis

Sequences from the ITS region and partial region of the ACT gene should only be compared to P. ampelicida ex-neotype strain ATCC 200578, GenBank accession KC193586 (ITS) and KC193581 (ACT). For a positive identification, the ITS sequence of the sample must be 100% match to the reference sequence, while ACT must be 99-100% match.
