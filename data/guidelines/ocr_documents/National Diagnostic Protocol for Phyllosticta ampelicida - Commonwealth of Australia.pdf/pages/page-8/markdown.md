NDP 13 V2 - National Diagnostic Protocol for Phyllosticta ampelicida

# Descriptions from Zhang et al. (2013) based on neotype.

Pycnidia black or blackish brown, globose or subglobose, 150–300 mm diam. on PDA media.

Conidiogenous cells cylindrical or conical, 9–13(–15) x 3–4.5 mm. Conidia hyaline one-celled, broadly ovoid, ellipsoidal, seldom subglobose, some with a truncate base, 8.5–12.5 x 6–7.5 mm, surrounded by a slime layer, containing small green guttules, with short appendages, 2–7 mm. Spermatia hyaline, one-celled, dumbbell shaped, 5–7 x 1.3–2.2 mm.

Culture characteristics: Colonies reaching 3 cm diam. in 7 d on PDA, pale gray to olive green when mature, gray olivaceous to greenish black in reverse. Aerial mycelium dense, white; submerged mycelium olivaceous to brown. Stromata formed after 20 d, black.
